Employers

Awareness and Sensitization

Online workshops by Neuro diverse team of facilitators to enhance awareness & empathy. These also emphasize on the “what’s in it for me” to employees and identify who would be interested in forming a core ND Employee Support Group.

Read More

ND Ally Certification

Focus groups with ESG members, team members, Team Leads and Managers of ND Hires. These ensure safe work environments. Are conducted by ND Co Trainers to provide hands on experience of working in neurodiverse teams, through simulations.

Read More

Hiring

Competency mapping of targeted roles and identification of Behavioural Indicators. Competency assessment tools designed to assess and screen ND individuals. Partner with Hiring companies to identify the right fits.

Read More

Coaching

One on one and group coaching interventions with ND employees to ensure they are well integrated. Individual Development Plans are laid down, progress reviewed and feedback is shared with employees and their Managers.

Read More

Employee Parent Support

Platform for employees, whose children are neurodivergent. Webinars to empower them and address parenting challenges. Build a self-help community within the organization, that results in employee engagement CSR initiatives in the ND space.
