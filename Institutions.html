Employability Support

This includes Career Mapping and Placement Services.
Career Mapping - Identify strengths and interests and do a career mapping of neurodiverse students who are in their XII or with Job seekers, using online tools and one on one interventions with our career coaches and counselors.
Placement Services (Internship/Jobs) - Identify the right roles based on individual expertise and aptitude, prepare for job readiness and bridge connects with ND friendly employers through our corporate network.

Awareness and Sensitization

Online workshops by Neuro diverse team of facilitators in academic institutes of higher education to enhance neurodiversity awareness & sensitization.
These include self-assessments of neurodivergent traits, shift to the social model, leveraging empathy and focusing on strengths.
